The Vasanthanarasapura Industrial Association (VIA) was established on November 18, 2013, under the Karnataka Society Registration Act of 1960. Its primary objective is to protect and promote the interests of industries in the Vasanthanarasapura area by providing a forum to address common challenges and collaborate with government departments.

  • The association has been instrumental in facilitating the development of key industrial projects in the region.
  • India's First Integrated Machine Tool Park: Completed in March 2021, this 530-acre park aims to enhance the competitiveness of the Indian capital goods sector..
  • Japan Industrial Township (JIT): Aimed at attracting Japanese companies to invest in the region, the JIT initiative underscores the association's role in fostering international business relations.

Through these initiatives, the Vasanthanarasapura Industrial Association continues to play a pivotal role in the economic development of the Tumakuru district and the broader Karnataka region.
